Veresliha (2020)

Veresliha is a fast-paced retro style FPS made for a school assignment in the autumn of 2020. We worked in a team of two people. My primary responsibility was programming the core gameplay mechanics.

All the 3D-assets and level design shown in the video are also done by me, as well as the soundtrack.

Notable features:

  • Two levels, approximitely 5 minutes of gameplay
  • Quake-style movement physics (bunnyhopping, airstrafing)
  • Five unique weapons and a quick melee attack that reflects projectiles
  • Basic predicative projectile shooting enemy AI (two different enemy variants)
