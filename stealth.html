Stealth Game Enemy AI (2021)

A stealth game enemy AI I made using Unreal Engine 4 during the summer of 2021.

Notable features:

  • Player has a light amount variable which affects the enemy behaviour (e.g. the enemy cannot see player hiding in shadows)
  • Enemy has localized damage system and multiple injure states.
  • Enemy will pick weapon up with his left hand if he drops it due to damage to right hand.
  • Injure state affects how the enemy moves (limping/crawling/twitching on the ground)
  • Will flee if too vurnerable
